CoPlay Loadouts: Choosing the Right Configuration For Your Xbox Fleet

CoPlay

Jun 4, 2023 • 1 min read

Navigating the myriad of privacy and security options for your xboxes can be tricky. Luckily, we've developed what we call "Loadouts" to help simplify this process.

Loadouts are combination of user account and console settings that allow you to have full control over what users of your xbox fleet can do.

Some important settings that we have identified are the following:

  • Adding their own Xbox accounts
  • Changing settings
  • Purchasing games
  • Web surfing
  • Linking social media accounts
  • Resetting the Xbox
  • Playing Online
  • Sending messages
  • Voice Chatting
  • Adding Friends

We have created Loadouts to help you determine what settings you want available to your users. You can find our Loadouts here:

  • https://coplay.io/the-pro-loadout/
  • https://coplay.io/the-lockdown-loadout/

We recommend the Pro loadout as it allows your users to get the most of their xbox experience.
