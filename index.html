Our Mission

We are passionate about making the world a better place for people by empowering hospitals to use creative technology and gaming. We believe the power of play has the ability to ease suffering and loneliness and provide connection and joy. Our mission is to provide the tools and services needed to make these experiences as accessible as possible to patients and hospitals.

We Build Tools

Hospitals and other organizations lack the tools needed to deploy gaming technologies at scale in a way that is compliant with local laws and regulations. Many of these technologies were not built with enterprise settings in mind. At CoPlay, we fill in the gap.

To Help Kids

We believe that play is a fundamental part of a child's development. We are building tools to help kids in hospitals and other organizations play and learn in a way that is safe and fun.

Enjoy Their Hospital Stay

Hospitals don’t have to be a scary place. We build tools that help provide a sense of normalcy and peace for patients and families through the power of play. Join us as we build technology for good.
